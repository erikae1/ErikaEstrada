Hobbies

Most of my day consists of studying and school, so I have a lot of hobbies that help me feel relaxed. I really enjoy running and exercising, I feel at peace because I am doing something that benefits my body now and in the future. In the fall it was Cross Country season and I joined it because it seemed fun; I really enjoyed it. I was running 2-3 miles everyday, training and taking my time. We would train everyday to race in meets on Thrusday, it was so fun, but a little physically draining. I was running so much and so often that my body couldn't keep up, so I ended up with a bad knee and hip. At fisrt the pain started as soreness, then developed into unbearable pain, it was so bad that I couldn't walk good for a few days. Luckily, I had my sisters who were also runners, so they helped me tape up my knee and hip, which helped keep the nerve and muscle in place. I was limping the first few days, but with the tape, I got better. Now, I still go on runs to continue being in shape for this incoming season. I also do a lot of excerscise and streches so I don't get hurt again. I go to the gym often with my sisters, like 5 days a week. I love the gym because I like knowing that I am working towards being healthy. It's so fun going with my sisters because we have deep talks.

Another one of my hobbies is reading, I love reading! Reading is so fun because I imagine myself in the story and forget about what is going on around me, it's a way to escape reality. I love cheesy romcoms and fantasy actions. I really enjoy books, manwhua, and webtoons. Reading about love, action, family, friends, royalty, and so many other things makes me happy and excited for when it's my turn to experience somthing like that.
